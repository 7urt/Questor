# Privacy Policy for Questor

Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

## 1. Data Collection
We collect the following data to provide our services:
- **Discord User IDs**: To identify your account and link configurations.
- **Discord Tokens**: Only if you link your account for quest automation.
- **Bot Usage Data**: Basic logs to monitor performance and fix bugs.

## 2. Data Usage
The collected data is used exclusively for:
- Automating Discord Quests.
- Managing HypeSquad badges and booster perks.
- Providing notifications for new quests.
We do **not** sell or share your data with third parties for marketing purposes.

## 3. Data Protection
We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your stored tokens and personal information. Data is stored in a secure MongoDB Atlas cluster.

## 4. User Rights
You have the right to:
- View the data we have stored about you.
- Request the deletion of your data at any time using the provided bot commands (e.g., `,unlink`).
- Stop using the service and revoke your token's access.

## 5. Third-Party Services
Questor interacts with the Discord API. Your use of Discord is subject to Discord's own Privacy Policy and Terms of Service.

## 6. Updates to Policy
We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify users of significant changes via our support server or bot announcements.

## 7. Contact Information
For any privacy-related concerns, please visit our support server: https://discord.gg/questor
